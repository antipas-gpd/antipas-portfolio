Phishing Prevention Tips

  • Check the sender's email address carefully for slight misspellings
  • Hover over links to see the actual URL before clicking
  • Be wary of urgent requests for personal information
  • Look for poor grammar and spelling mistakes
  • Verify suspicious emails by contacting the organization directly
  • Use multi-factor authentication for important accounts
